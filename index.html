About

The Institute

Jaypee Institute of Information Technology (JIIT), Noida was established in the year 2001 and has been constantly ranked among the top engineering Institutes in Delhi NCR. JIIT has been accredited and approved by AICTE, NIRF, MHRD and has continued to become a centre of excellence in emerging areas of science and technology.

The Department

The Department of Biotechnology at JIIT, NOIDA established in 2002, remains committed to provide research-informed teaching and learning, and vibrant R&D environment. Faculty with rich research exposure in academia and industry, both in India and abroad, contributes to the department academic core.

The Conference

“5th International Conference on Advances in Biosciences and Biotechnology (ICABB-2022)” on Innovations in life sciences and computational biology from 20 to 22 Jan, 2022 will be an amalgamation of multi-disciplinary fields of Biotechnology providing opportunities for exchange of ideas, scientific knowledge and experience amongst researchers, academicians, scientists and students from Biological, Medical, Environmental, Agricultural and Computational fields. The scientific program will include plenary/ keynote lectures along with oral and poster presentation under 5 major themes focusing on breakthroughs in Biological Sciences, Computational approaches in Biotechnology, Genetics, Environmental, Microbial and Agricultural Biotechnology as well as, other upcoming current technological advancements in the field of Lifesciences.

FAQs

How many posters can I present in a given single payment?

You are allowed to present a maximum of 2 papers and you’ll receive the certificates for the same. If in case you are willing to present more than 2 papers or you have your name in other papers as well for which you wish to receive the certificate, you’ll have to go for a second payment.

What is the format of the poster?

The details for the format of poster are given in the guidelines section. Click here

What is the format of the abstract?

The details for the format of abstract are given in the guidelines section. Click here

What will be the duration for poster/oral presentation?

For duration of the presentation and other details kindly go through the guidelines section. Click here

What all proceedings an attendee is entitled to join?

All the attendees will have access to all the presentations, sessions and the talks by the keynote speakers throughout the conference.

What is the payment procedure?

After you register you will receive a confirmation mail which will contain your payment amount, the link for the payment and your participant ID.

In case of poster/oral presentations, each participant is required to make the payment individually.

How will I receive my confirmation for payment?

All participants will receive confirmation of payment through mail.

By when will I receive my participation/appreciation certificate?

All participants and winners will receive their respective certificates within 2-3 working days post conference.

By when will I receive the conference schedule?

The schedule of the conference will be available on the website and you’ll receive the copy of the same on your registered email ID 2 days before the conference i.e. 26 th January 2021.

By when will I receive the conference proceedings?

The conference proceedings will be provided to you on your registered email ID post conference.

In which journal paper will my abstract be published?

We are planning to publish the conference proceedings (only abstracts) in esteemed journal ‘Asian Journal of Pharmaceutical and Clinical Research’.

Is there any chance that I can publish my full-length paper?

Yes, we are planning to publish full-length manuscripts as mentioned below:

1. Scopus indexed ‘Current Trends in Biotechnology and Pharmacy’

2. The Pharmaceutical and Chemical Journal

The publishing of paper is subject to review and payment.
